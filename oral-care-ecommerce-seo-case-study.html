The Story

I had the privilege of working with Ordo Life, a specialized e-commerce store in the oral care niche. As they sought to dominate a highly competitive market, my goal was clear: to drive substantial organic traffic to their site and establish them as a leading player in the oral care industry.

Client Challenge

Ordo Life faced several challenges that are typical for niche e-commerce stores. Despite offering a range of high-quality oral care products, their online visibility was limited, and organic traffic was stagnant. They needed to stand out in a crowded market, improve search engine rankings, and attract high-intent buyers. The challenge wasn’t just to increase traffic but to ensure that this traffic converted into sales.

My Strategy

To address these challenges, I implemented a strategic SEO plan:

  • Website Audit: Firstly, I started with a thorough audit to identify and rectify technical issues. This included optimizing site speed, ensuring a seamless mobile experience, and enhancing the overall user experience to reduce bounce rates and improve conversion rates.
  • Keyword Research: Next, I conducted in-depth keyword research focused on the oral care niche. I identified high-value, purchase-intent keywords that competitors had overlooked, ensuring that Ordo Life ranked for terms that would attract ready-to-buy customers.
  • On-Page Optimization: I optimized product pages, category pages, and the site’s overall structure. This involved improving meta titles, descriptions, and internal linking, ensuring that each product was easily discoverable by both search engines and potential customers.
  • Content Optimization: Content is king, even in e-commerce. I revamped existing content and developed new, informative blog posts and product descriptions that were rich in the identified keywords. This not only improved search rankings but also educated consumers, helping to establish Ordo Life as a trusted authority in oral care.
  • Off-Page SEO: Finally, I implemented a robust off-page SEO strategy. This included building high-quality backlinks through partnerships with dental health blogs, guest posting, and engaging in social media outreach to further boost Ordo Life’s online authority.

The Results

After implementing the SEO strategy, the client saw strong growth in organic performance and higher keyword visibility. Key results included:

  • 85% increase in organic traffic
  • 72% increase in organic Session
  • 75% increase in organic traffic Value
  • 150% increase in keywords ranking in top 10 of SERP

Within a few months, we saw clear growth in traffic and rankings, and the quality of visitors improved too."
