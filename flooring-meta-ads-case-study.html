The Story

I worked with a flooring business that wanted to grow its customer base using Facebook Ads. They had struggled to run profitable campaigns in the past and needed a structured approach to generate consistent, high-quality leads while controlling costs.

Client Challenge

The client faced several challenges: weak brand presence, difficulty reaching the right audience, and high lead acquisition costs. They needed a Facebook Ads strategy that could deliver qualified leads while remaining cost-effective and scalable.

My Strategy

To overcome these challenges, I implemented a data-driven Facebook Ads strategy focused on lead quality and efficiency:

  • Buyer Persona Creation: Defined ideal customer profiles for homeowners and contractors.
  • Campaign Strategy: Built campaigns aligned with business goals.
  • Audience Research: Identified high-intent interest and behavior segments.
  • Audience Segmentation: Segmented audiences by interest and location.
  • Ad Creatives & Copy: Designed engaging creatives with strong CTAs.
  • Lead Form Optimization: Optimized Facebook lead forms for higher completion rates.
  • Bidding Strategy: Used highest volume bidding for consistent lead flow.
  • Performance Monitoring: Continuously monitored and optimized campaigns.
  • Scaling: Increased budget on high-performing ad sets.

The Results

The Facebook Ads campaign delivered strong results across all key metrics:

  • 78 Facebook lead form leads generated
  • 328 website leads generated
  • 29% decrease in cost per lead
  • $29 average cost per lead
  • 1.89M total reach

The campaign successfully increased brand visibility, delivered high-quality leads, and maintained cost efficiency, making it scalable for future growth.
