The Story

I collaborated with a local plumbing business in the USA that wanted to boost online visibility and generate more leads in their service areas. The main goal was to drive organic traffic and improve local search rankings, which is essential for getting customers in a competitive local market.

Client Challenge

Like many local service providers, the business was well-known in the community, but their online presence was weak. They struggled to rank in local search results and needed not only more traffic, but highly local traffic—people who were ready to book plumbing services.

My Strategy

Since local SEO is critical for this type of business, I built a strategy focused on both on-site and off-site improvements:

  • Website Audit: Identified technical issues, improved site speed, ensured mobile-friendliness, and enhanced user experience to increase conversions.
  • Keyword Research: Focused on location-based search terms and high-intent keywords people use when they need a plumber in the service area.
  • On-Page Optimization: Optimized service and contact pages with local keywords, refined titles/descriptions/headings, and improved internal linking for better navigation and relevance.
  • Local SEO Optimization: Optimized the Google Business Profile, ensured consistent NAP across directories, built local citations, and encouraged customer reviews to strengthen local rankings.
  • Content Optimization: Created and improved helpful content (common plumbing problems, maintenance tips) using location keywords to increase local visibility.
  • Off-Page SEO: Earned backlinks from reputable local sites and industry directories, supported with social outreach to grow local awareness.

The Results

After the SEO strategy was implemented, the client saw strong improvements in organic performance and local visibility:

  • 76% increase in organic traffic
  • 67% increase in organic sessions
  • 65% increase in organic traffic value
  • 130% increase in keywords ranking in top 10 of SERP

The traffic was not only higher in volume but also more relevant, leading to a noticeable increase in service bookings and customer inquiries from the targeted local area.

"We were getting work through referrals, but online leads were inconsistent and local rankings were not strong. After the SEO improvements, we started showing up more often for the right searches in our service areas. We noticed a real lift in calls and booking inquiries coming from organic traffic."
