The Story

I worked with a real estate business looking to increase their online visibility in a highly competitive market. The primary goal was to drive organic traffic and attract qualified leads by optimizing their website and focusing on local search, which is critical for real estate success.

Client Challenge

The client faced the challenge of improving overall online visibility while also appearing prominently in local search results. Despite being an established business, their organic presence was weak, and they struggled to attract local leads. The challenge was not just increasing traffic, but driving highly targeted visitors that converted into inquiries and appointments.

My Strategy

To address these challenges, I implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy combining general SEO and local optimization techniques:

  • Website Audit: Identified and fixed technical issues, improved site speed, ensured mobile responsiveness, and enhanced overall user experience.
  • Keyword Research: Conducted in-depth research to identify high-value real estate keywords, including location-based search terms used by buyers and renters.
  • On-Page Optimization: Optimized key pages such as property listings and service pages with refined titles, descriptions, headings, and improved internal linking.
  • Local SEO Optimization: Optimized Google Business Profile, ensured NAP consistency across directories, built local citations, and encouraged client reviews to strengthen local rankings.
  • Content Optimization: Created and optimized location-focused content, including neighborhood guides, market trends, and real estate advice blogs.
  • Off-Page SEO: Built high-quality backlinks from reputable real estate and local websites and supported growth through social outreach.

The Results

After implementing the SEO strategy, the client experienced strong growth in both organic and local search performance:

  • 89% increase in organic traffic
  • 80% increase in organic sessions
  • 78% increase in organic traffic value
  • 170% increase in keywords ranking in top 10 of SERP

"We wanted to generate more local inquiries but weren’t getting enough visibility online. The SEO strategy was well-structured and focused on the right areas. Over time, we saw a clear increase in traffic, better local rankings, and more qualified leads coming through our website."
